## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Account Registration:**
- Full name
- Email address
- Date of birth (for age verification; we require users to be 18 years or older)
- University/College/School name
- Athletic sport and position
- Athletic year (Freshman, Sophomore, Junior, Senior, Graduate Student, Other)
- Password (hashed and encrypted; we never store plain-text passwords)
- Profile bio and training availability
- Avatar/profile photo

**Verification Documents (Optional):**
- Student ID photo or school email (uploaded to secure cloud storage)
- Team roster screenshot (showing your name)
- School athletic department confirmation letter/email
- These are processed and deleted after verification (within 7 days)

**Communications:**
- Training clips (videos you upload to the Service)
- Chat messages with other athletes
- Feedback, support requests, or reports you submit

**Payment Information:**
- If we introduce premium features in the future, payment data will be processed by a third-party payment processor (Stripe or equivalent); we do not store credit card information directly.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Location Data:**
- When you grant permission, we collect your precise GPS location (latitude/longitude) to:
  - Calculate distance between you and other athletes
  - Show "athletes near me" in the Discover tab
  - Enable local training partner matching
- You can revoke location permissions at any time in your device settings; Projectory will function without location data (distance matching will be unavailable)
- We do not sell or share your location with third parties

**Device Information:**
- Device type, operating system, and version
- Device identifier (IDFA, Android Advertising ID)
- IP address
- Browser/app version
- Crash logs and error reports (via Firebase Crashlytics)

**Usage Analytics:**
- Pages/screens visited and time spent
- Features used (e.g., "viewed athlete profile," "sent message," "posted clip")
- Search queries
- Connection requests sent/received
- Account actions (login, logout, profile edits)
- We use Firebase Analytics to track this data

**Cookies & Similar Technologies:**
- We do not currently use cookies, but may implement them for authentication and user preferences. Any use will be disclosed in an updated Privacy Policy.

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

- **NCAA Verification Services:** If we partner with the NCAA Eligibility Center or similar verification providers, they may share confirmation of your D1 athlete status (name, sport, school only)
- **Social Media:** If you sign in via social media (future feature), we collect data per that platform's terms
- **Other Athletes:** When users report, block, or interact with you, we may receive information about you in that context

## 5. Data Security

### 5.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your data:

- **Encryption in Transit:** All data sent to/from Projectory is encrypted using TLS 1.2+ (HTTPS)
- **Encryption at Rest:** Sensitive data (passwords, verification documents) is encrypted in our database
- **Password Hashing:** Passwords are hashed using bcrypt or similar; we cannot decrypt them
- **Access Controls:** Only authorized employees can access user data
- **Regular Audits:** We conduct security audits and penetration testing
- **Incident Response Plan:** If a breach occurs, we will notify affected users within 30 days

### 5.2 Your Responsibility

You are responsible for:
- Keeping your password confidential
- Not sharing your account credentials
- Logging out after use, especially on shared devices
- Reporting suspicious activity immediately

**We are not responsible for unauthorized access resulting from your failure to secure your password.**

## 6. Your Rights & Control

### 6.1 Access & Portability

You have the right to:
- Access all personal data we hold about you
- Download your data in a portable format (CSV, JSON)
- Request this at any time via support@projectoryathletes.app

### 6.2 Correction & Updates

You can update your profile information at any time within the app. If you identify inaccurate data, contact us to correct it.

### 6.3 Deletion & Account Closure

You have the absolute right to delete your account. When you request account deletion:
- Your profile is removed from Projectory within 7 days
- Your chat messages are deleted
- Your clips are deleted
- Your personal data is removed from our database
- Verification documents are securely deleted
- Aggregate analytics (e.g., "1 soccer player from UNC") may be retained

**You can delete your account any time from Settings → Delete Account.** No reason required; no questions asked.

### 6.4 Opt-Out of Marketing

You can opt out of promotional emails by:
- Clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any email
- Changing notification settings in the app
- Contacting us at support@projectoryathletes.app

### 6.5 Location Permission Control

You can revoke location permission in your device settings (iOS: Settings → Privacy → Location; Android: Settings → Permissions → Location). Projectory will continue to work without location data.

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|---|---|
